FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How long does it take to set up?

Less than 5 minutes. Seriously. It's a single integration point.

Will this work with [insert your tech stack]?

Yes. Agent Smith only needs an email address as input and delivers email copy as output. It works with any system.

What if I change email platforms or CRMs?

No problem. Agent Smith doesn't care what systems you use. Email in, personalized message out.

Do I need technical skills to implement this?

If you can copy and paste, you have all the technical skills required.

How does Agent Smith learn about my leads?

It uses the domain from their business email to find and analyze their website, social profiles, and other public information.

Can I customize what Agent Smith looks for when researching?

Absolutely. You can define the research scope (homepage only or all pages), specific sections to focus on (about us, team, products), and what information matters most to your business.

Can I control how the emails are written?

Yes. You can customize the tone, style, and length of generated emails. You can also create company-specific templates with variable substitution to maintain brand consistency.

Will I have to pay more as I grow?

Never. One payment covers unlimited usage. Your only potential cost is OpenAI API tokens, which you control directly.
